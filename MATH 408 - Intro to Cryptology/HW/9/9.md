## 3.1

Solve $x^{73}=714 \pmod{1159}$

> [!answer]
> $1159=19\times61$
> $x=11\pmod{19}$
> $x^{13}=43\pmod{61}$
> 
> ---
> 
> $13^{-1}\pmod{60}$
> ![[tables#^1]]
> 
> $x=43^{37}\pmod{61}$
> $x=59\pmod{61}$
> 
> ---
> 
> $x=11\pmod{19}$
> $x=59\pmod{61}$
> 
> $x=11+19k$
> $11+19k=59\pmod{61}$
> $19k=48\pmod{61}$
> ![[tables#^2]]
> 
> $19^{-1}=45\pmod{61}$
> $k=25\pmod{61}$
> 
> $x=11+19(25+61m)\pmod{1159}$
> $x=486\pmod{1159}$
> $\square$

### b

Alice knows that her modulus factors into a product of two primes, one of which is $1301$ Find a decryption exponent $d$ for Alice.

> [!answer]
> $N=1301\times1567$
> $\phi(N)=1300\times1566=2035800$
