# Addition
Adding vectors is equivalent to adding elements component-wise
 $\vec{v}=\begin{bmatrix}a\\b\end{bmatrix}$
$\vec{w}=\begin{bmatrix}c\\d\end{bmatrix}$
$\vec{v}+\vec{w}=\begin{bmatrix}a+c\\b+d\end{bmatrix}$
