# Logic Laws

1. Communicative
	1. $p\lor q\iff q\lor p$
	2. $p\land q\iff q\land p$
2. Associative
	1. $(p\lor q)\lor r\iff p\lor (q\lor r)$
	2. $(p\land q)\land r\iff p\land (q\land r)$
3. Distributive
	1. $p\land(q\lor r)\iff(p\land q)\lor(p\land r)$
