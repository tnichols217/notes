## 2

$\frac{dy}{dt}=\frac{(t^2-1)(y^2-2)}{y^2-4}$

> [!answer]

# 3

$\frac{dP}{dt}=0.4P\left( 1-\frac{P}{230} \right)$

## a

When is $P$ in equilibrium

> [!answer]
> $P=0,230$

## b

When is  $P$ increasing

> [!answer]
> $0<P<230$

## c

When is $P$ decreasing

> [!answer]
> $P<0,P>230$
