$\vec r(t)=\langle\cos t,\sin t\rangle$
$t=0$

$\nabla f(\vec r(t))=\langle3,-7\rangle$
$\vec r'(t)=\langle-\sin t,\cos t\rangle$

$\nabla f(\vec r(t))\cdot \vec r'(t)=-3\sin t-7\cos t$
$\nabla f(\vec r(0))\cdot \vec r'(0)=-7$
$\square$
