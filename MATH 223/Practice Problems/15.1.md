$\int\limits_0^4\int\limits_0^5(x+y)^{-1/2}~dydx$

$=\int\limits_0^4\bigg|_0^52(x+y)^{1/2}~dydx$
$=\int\limits_0^42((x+5)^{1/2}-x^{1/2})~dx$
$=\bigg|_0^44((x+5)^{3/2}-x^{3/2})/3~dx$
$=4((9)^{3/2}-(4)^{3/2}-(5)^{3/2})/3$
$=4(27-8-5\sqrt5)/3$
$=4(19-5\sqrt5)/3$
$\approx10.426$
