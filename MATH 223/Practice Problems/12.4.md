$=\langle1,-3,2\rangle\times\langle0,1,-1\rangle$
$=\begin{vmatrix}\hat{i}&\hat{j}&\hat{k}\\1&-3&2\\0&1&-1\end{vmatrix}$

$=4\langle1,1,0\rangle$
