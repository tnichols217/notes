# Probability Theory

## Set Theory

> [!def]
> **Sample Space**
> All possible outcomes of a variable, often denoted as **$S$**

Finite sample spaces can be denoted with all elements inside a list enclosed by curly braces.
$S=\{H,T\}$ for the sample space of flipping a coin
