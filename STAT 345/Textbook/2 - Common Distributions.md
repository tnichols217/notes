# Exponential Distribution

For $\beta>0$
$\text{Expo}(\beta)=\begin{cases}\frac{1}{\beta}e^{-x/\beta}&,x\ge0\\0&,x<0\end{cases}$

# Gamma Distribution

For $\alpha>0,\beta>0$
$\text{Gamma}(\alpha,\beta)=\begin{cases}\frac{1}{\Gamma(\alpha)\beta^\alpha}x^{\alpha-1}e^{-x/\beta}&,x\ge0\\0&,x<0\end{cases}$
